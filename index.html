How we met

Rachel and Aaron first bonded over their shared love of blues dancing and silly board games. After Rachel attended several game nights at Aaron's house, their friend Katy decided to set them up. This required her to whisper/yell at both of them individually in order to get their first date organized. Their first date lasted approximately 13 and 1/2 hours. Their wedding will be a comparatively short five-hour affair.
