We got married!

On May 30th, 2020 Rachel and Aaron were married in their backyard on a gorgeous late spring morning in Blacksburg, VA. While it wasn't the wedding that we had originally planned, it was a lovely day with family and friends - both near and far. Rachel and Aaron would like to thank all the people that attended both in person and remote over zoom. If you would like to reach out to the couple feel free to e-mail Aaron or Rachel.

How we met

Rachel and Aaron first bonded over their shared love of blues dancing and silly board games. After Rachel attended several game nights at Aaron's house, their friend Katy decided to set them up. This required her to whisper/yell at both of them individually in order to get their first date organized. Their first date lasted approximately 13 and 1/2 hours. Their wedding was a comparatively short affair.
